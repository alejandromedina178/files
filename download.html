Living In Rosslyn Arlington, Va | Best Homes, Condos, Fun, And Dining | Moving To Arlington 2021

January 29, 2021

These streets are home to Nestle chocolate – but that’s not the sweetest part of this neighborhood! Join us today as we give you a full breakdown of Rosslyn, one of our favorite neighborhoods in Arlington, Virginia.

No matter where you live in Arlington VA, there’s something unique to fall in love with! What do you love about your community? Let us know down in the comments below, so we can highlight them in a future Neighborhood Spotlight. 

And, as always, if you have any questions about Arlington real estate, please click here to contact the Keri Shull Team, the #1 top-selling real estate team in the Washington, DC area. 

Where is Rosslyn?

Rosslyn is a thriving community that sits right on the Potomac River between DC and Virginia in Northern Arlington VA. The neighborhood is well-known for its amazing views of the iconic DC skyline, as well as its urban atmosphere. 

It’s an especially quick trip into DC or to destinations in Northern Virginia (NoVa) thanks to Rosslyn’s easy access to the Washington Metro’s Blue, Silver, and Orange Lines via Rosslyn station. This also puts Rosslyn within easy reach of all the restaurants, arts venues, shops, and nightlife across DC.

What makes people want to live in Rosslyn?

Rosslyn is ideal for people who want the true Urban Village experience that is so unique to Arlington, Virginia. In addition to having tons of entertainment and employment opportunities within Rosslyn itself, residents can also access DC with ease via the Rosslyn Metro station or over the Key Bridge. 

So, if you feel like popping over to historic Georgetown for a bit of shopping or a quick bite, living in Rosslyn makes that easy! Rosslyn is also very convenient to shopping, dining, and working options across the rest of Northern Arlington. With world-class public transit options and incredibly high walkability, residents never have to worry about missing out on what this amazing city has to offer!

Things to Do in Rosslyn

If you’re looking for things to do in Arlington VA, Rosslyn not only offers you all of DC – it also has a lot to offer right in your neighborhood.

Some of our favorite destinations in Rosslyn include the memorials and public art: Places like Dark Star Park, Gateway Park, and Roosevelt Island are great places to get some air.

Great restaurants in Rosslyn include:

  • Quarterdeck Restaurant
  • Savor Salvadoran food truck
  • Barley Mac
  • Quinn’s on the Corner
  • And more!

Check out our Definitive Guide to Rosslyn, Virginia | Local Streets and Eats video to see some of the best dishes available at these restaurants:

Another spot we love is The View of DC, a 31st-floor observation deck with 360-degree views of DC and all of Arlington. The View of DC is open to all Arlington residents free of charge.

Living in Rosslyn, Arlington VA

Living in Rosslyn means living in the gateway connecting DC and Virginia, giving you the best of both!

When it comes to types of homes for sale, Rosslyn is known for its condos. Highrises dot the skyline, many of which are filled with urban, apartment-style condominiums. These homes offer super high walkability, and many of them are perfect for luxury living in Arlington.

Rosslyn also features a thriving job market. Nestle has its American headquarters in Rosslyn, and even tech giant Microsoft just signed a lease for a new sales center in the neighborhood.

So, why are homes in such demand in Rosslyn? Here’s just a couple of reasons why:

  • Lightning-fast commutes to big employers such as Nestle
  • Great public transportation via the Rosslyn Metro Stop
  • Close proximity to DC over the Key Bridge, especially to neighborhoods like Georgetown and Foggy Bottom
  • Stunning views of the District and North Arlington
  • Fantastic dining, shopping, and entertainment in Rosslyn itself

Given how fast the housing market moves in Arlington, it can be difficult to find and win your dream home in Rosslyn. That’s why it’s so important to work with a real estate team that can streamline your search and help negotiate for you. In fact, the best thing to do is work with a team that can help find you “hidden gem” off-market homes in Rosslyn!

Popular Rosslyn Communities

The Rosslyn real estate market is defined by its condominium buildings. Here are some of our favorite communities — make sure to keep an eye on these!

The Pierce

The Pierce is the newest addition to the Rosslyn skyline — and what an addition it is! This hyper-modern, hyper-luxurious condo community stands at a towering 27 stories. It is still undergoing final construction at the time of recording, but the units are currently for sale. 

Floor plans include 1-bedroom with den, 2-bedroom, and 2-bedroom with den. 

Gaslight Square

The condos at Gaslight Square are some of the most unique in Rosslyn. With soaring ceilings and an old-world aesthetic, these homes offer a distinctly loft-like atmosphere. Each condominium unit features its own private outdoor space, so you can enjoy a slice of privacy in the heart of Rosslyn.

Key & Nash

Key & Nash is a newer addition to the Rosslyn market, having opened to the public in 2017. This 6-story building features 63 condo units, ranging from 1- to 3-bedroom floor plans. Residents have access to a rooftop terrace, underground parking, private courtyard, concierge, fitness center, billiards lounge, and bike storage.  

Turnberry Tower

Turnberry Tower has 247 units across 26 stories, with options for 1- to 4- bedroom floorplans. Each condo has 9-12 foot ceilings, top-quality Miele cooking appliances, Sub-Zero refrigerators, and high-end finishes. These condos also have floor-to-ceiling windows, so you can have the best view possible of DC and Arlington.
